Sajjan Raj Vaidya

Sajjan Raj Vaidya is a Nepalese singer, songwriter, guitarist, composer, and producer based in New York City. He released his debut single, "Aaja Matra", in 2014[1] and rose to prominence after the release of his singles "Hawaijahaj" (2016)[2][3][4] and "Chitthi Bhitra" (2018).[2][5] One of his most widely known singles is "Hataarindai, Bataasindai"

His release, titled "BLYHL", was released in February 2020. His song, "Phutki Janey Jovan" released on 16 May 2020 shows various clips of people during the COVID-19 pandemic all around the world, with a special emphasis on Nepali people.[8] He then released "Lukaamaari" on January 1, 2021. After 3 months of "Lukaamaari", he released his next song, "Ultaa Paailaa". He also released the songs "Parkhaai" and "Naganya Maya" by the while, he released his two new singles "Sasto Mutu" and "Ek Sarvanaam" at the beginning of 2022. And recently his new single "Dhairya" released on Oct 21, 2022 shows the clips of his live shows done in Nepal Tour.
